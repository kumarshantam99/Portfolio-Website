About Me

I am currently pursuing MS in Computer Science with concentration in Data Science. Machine Learning is one of the most fascinating subjects that I took as part of my coursework and its integration with Web Development opens an endless world of ideas for developers.

I was born in Bihar, India - this state has two of the world's oldest universities- Nalanda University and Vikramshila University (above image). Being the eldest son in a family of doctors, I was expected to pursue biology but I knew I wanted to pursue Physics more than anything. Preparing for IIT-JEE examination, Physics Olympiads, pursuing an undergraduate degree in ECE and then finding coding- my educational journey has been one roller coaster ride. I discovered Machine Learning when I came to the US for my Master's, and I will say it was love at first sight ( WHAT A NERD!!)

Monday 9/14/2020

This day marks a new beginning in my life: My internship starts from today. I know its an unpaid position, but mark my words- you are going to love the work there. For the first time in my life, I am driven- driven to learn, driven to code and driven to grow. In the movie Steve Jobs, Steve played by Michael Fassbender, says "Dont be the musician playing in the opera, be the conductor." To that I say, " I would rather be a rockstar than be a conductor." I have the nerves to be reckless, I have the mindset to take the risks. My attitude has always defined my altitude. Pragya said it right- Last year I was crying and thinking about how I will go back to India and move to a different country, today I have a CPT approved and I am doing an internship, developing an algorithm. Mom, Dad, I want to say this to you- The time for you guys to relax and enjoy life is coming, I am going to take over the responsibilities of the family now.
